How to Prevent Fires

Unplugging

Unplug any unnecessary plugs, doing this will prevent any of them from being drawn into a flammable item/surface. It is still possible for things to catch on fire even when the plug is not connected to any device.

Manage Cables

Faulty wiring in devices can cause fires especially when they are not properly managed. There's also a higher chance when a cable is not properly secured in its place, it may also cause accidents like tripping on the cables.

Have a Fire Extinguisher

The fire extinguisher is the most simple and effective way in dealing with a fire by yourself. It is incredibly important to have at least one or two fire extinguishers inside your house.

Install a Smoke Detector

Smoke detectors is good way to detecting a fire before it spreads and grows. It will be a quick way to alert you or anyone in the house/building if there is a fire that had recently start.
